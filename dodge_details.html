Dodge Challenger

Model: Dodge Challenger

Price Range: Starts from around $28,000 for base models, up to $80,000+ for high-performance variants

Engine Options:

  • 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine producing around 305 horsepower and 268 lb-ft of torque
  • 5.7L HEMI V8 engine generating approximately 375 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque
  • 6.4L HEMI V8 engine (Scat Pack) delivering around 485 horsepower and 475 lb-ft of torque
  • Supercharged 6.2L HEMI V8 engine (Hellcat) producing an impressive 717 horsepower and 656 lb-ft of torque
  • Supercharged 6.2L HEMI V8 engine (Hellcat Redeye) pushing out an astounding 797 horsepower and 707 lb-ft of torque
  • Supercharged 6.2L HEMI V8 engine (Super Stock) producing an incredible 807 horsepower and 707 lb-ft of torque
  • Supercharged 6.2L HEMI V8 engine (SRT Demon) with up to 840 horsepower and 770 lb-ft of torque

Performance (0-60 mph): Ranges from approximately 3.5 seconds for high-performance variants to over 6 seconds for base models

Top Speed: Varies depending on the engine and model, typically ranging from around 130 mph for base models to over 200 mph for high-performance variants

Transmission: Available with 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic transmission options, depending on the model

Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD)

Description: The Dodge Challenger is a quintessential American muscle car known for its bold styling, powerful performance, and nostalgic appeal. It offers a wide range of engine options, allowing buyers to choose the level of performance that suits their preferences. From the entry-level models to the high-performance SRT variants, the Challenger delivers exhilarating driving experiences and a distinctively aggressive presence on the road.

Key Features:

  • Iconic and aggressive exterior design with retro styling cues
  • Powerful engine lineup offering thrilling performance across the range
  • Comfortable and well-appointed interior with modern amenities and advanced technology
  • Multiple trim levels and customization options available to suit various preferences
  • Performance-oriented suspension and braking systems for enhanced driving dynamics
  • Driver-focused features such as launch control and line lock for track-ready performance

Supra MK4 Details

Model: Supra MK4

Price: $130,000

Engine: 2JZ-GTE 3.0L inline-six twin-turbocharged engine

Power: 320 hp

Torque: 315 lb-ft

0-60 mph: Approximately 4.6 seconds

Top Speed: Approximately 155 mph (electronically limited)

Transmission: 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic

Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive

Description: The Toyota Supra MK4, also known as the Toyota Supra A80, is a legendary sports car celebrated for its performance, sleek design, and robust aftermarket support. Originally produced from 1993 to 2002, it gained widespread popularity among car enthusiasts and became an icon in the automotive world.

  • Iconic twin-turbocharged 2JZ-GTE engine
  • Legendary reliability and durability
  • Aggressive styling with sleek aerodynamics
  • Premium interior with comfortable seating and modern amenities

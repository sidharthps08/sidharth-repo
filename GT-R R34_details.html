Nissan Skyline GT-R R34

Model: Nissan Skyline GT-R R34

Price: $120,000

Engine: RB26DETT 2.6L twin-turbocharged inline-six

Power: 276 hp (officially limited due to the Japanese "gentlemen's agreement," but actual output is higher)

Torque: 293 lb-ft (397 Nm)

0-60 mph: Approximately 4.6 seconds

Top Speed: Electronically limited to 180 km/h (112 mph) due to Japanese regulations; unrestricted versions can reach over 320 km/h (200 mph)

Transmission: 6-speed Getrag manual

Drivetrain: ATTESA E-TS all-wheel drive

Description: The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is the fourth generation of the legendary GT-R series. It was produced from 1999 to 2002 and is highly regarded for its exceptional performance, advanced technology, and iconic status in the automotive world. The R34 GT-R is renowned for its impressive handling, acceleration, and tunability, making it a favorite among car enthusiasts and collectors.

Features:

  • Advanced ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system for superior traction and stability
  • Highly tunable RB26DETT engine with massive aftermarket support
  • Aggressive and iconic exterior design with functional aerodynamics
  • Advanced HICAS rear-wheel steering system for enhanced cornering performance
  • Performance-oriented Brembo brakes for exceptional stopping power
  • Multi-function display providing vital performance data
  • Comfortable and driver-focused interior with supportive bucket seats

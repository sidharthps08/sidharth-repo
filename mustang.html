Ford Mustang

Model: Ford Mustang

Price Range: Starting from $28,000 for base models, up to $70,000+ for high-performance variants

Engine Options:

  • 2.3L EcoBoost inline-4 engine producing around 310 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque
  • 5.0L Ti-VCT V8 engine generating approximately 450 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque
  • 5.2L V8 engine with Flat-Plane Crankshaft (Shelby GT350) delivering around 526 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque

Performance (0-60 mph): Ranges from approximately 4.5 seconds for base models to under 3.5 seconds for high-performance variants

Top Speed: Varies depending on the engine and model, typically ranging from around 130 mph for base models to over 180 mph for high-performance variants

Transmission: Available with 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic transmission options, depending on the model

Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD)

Description: The Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car known for its powerful performance, sleek design, and cultural significance. It offers a range of engine options, from fuel-efficient turbocharged engines to high-performance V8s, catering to a wide range of enthusiasts. With its unmistakable styling and exhilarating driving experience, the Mustang continues to be a symbol of freedom and individuality.

Key Features:

  • Iconic design with classic styling cues and modern touches
  • Powerful engine lineup offering a blend of performance and efficiency
  • Customization options including various trim levels, packages, and appearance upgrades
  • Advanced technology features such as SYNC infotainment system, FordPass Connect, and driver-assistance technologies
